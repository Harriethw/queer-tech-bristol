HTML DAY 2025

Join us for an HTML picnic

HTML Day is an annual celebration of HTML. It's a day to gather IRL in places around the world to write and learn HTML.

Celebrate for the first time in Bristol! We will have a low-key hang in the park to meet and talk about HTML. Bring a laptop if you’d like to work on something. We can provide sample projects if you’d like to learn to write HTML, or feel free to bring your own projects to share

If you’d like to take part in writing some HTML and creating a website together, bring along your laptop and a selection of images or text you’d like to share. We could create some digital gardens like this: https://htmlpoem.com

Check out the official HTML Day site for more info
